  2. AI Processing - Our system extracts all details (merchant, date, amount, tax, etc.) in under 30 seconds
  3. Choose Category - Select from preset categories like "Business Meal" or "Travel Expense" or write your own in custom
  4. Get Excel Report - Download organized spreadsheets with all your expenses and direct links to original receipts

No app downloads, no account setup, no learning curve. Just text and get results!

+

We support all common document formats:
